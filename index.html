Collision Vision

Collision Vision is a website provides drivers with an easy to use, interactive map, depicting all possible roads considered hazardous in given area. Based on previous crash data, our website provides predictions of what percent of the time a certain road will be considered "dangerous".

Collision Vision uses Microsoft Cognitive Services to identifty problematic intersections by training the model on satellite images taken of locations of crashes. The model then can recognize if the intersection is potentially dangerous.

Collision Vision was a collabrative project at TAMUHack 2020. I contributed by making the webapp and implementing the Google Maps API. My Team won Hacker Favorite.
